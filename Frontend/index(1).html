That pattern — dull pain, afternoons, tied to screen time, no fever — is most consistent with a tension-type headache, often from eye strain, posture, or dehydration.

A few things that usually help:

  • 20-20-20 rule: every 20 min, look 20 ft away for 20 sec
  • Check screen brightness and font size
  • Hydrate and stretch your neck/shoulders hourly
Low urgency
